About BetaCraft

BetaCraft is a dedicated Minecraft Beta 1.7.3 survival server that blends nostalgic gameplay with fresh mechanics.

  • A welcoming, tight-knit community
  • Active and engaged developers
  • Relaxed and friendly environment

Gameplay Features

  • Player-built towns
  • Economy driven by players
  • Ongoing community events
  • Frequent quality-of-life improvements

In-Game Rules

  • No griefing or destruction of others' builds
  • No harming animals (especially dogs) or stealing
  • Ask permission before building near another player's town
  • Be yourself and have fun!
  • No hacks, cheats, or x-ray mods
  • Rules are subject to change for community balance
